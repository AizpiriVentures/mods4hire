How It Works

Finding the right moderator is straightforward. Here's the process from start to hire.

1

Create a Listing

Describe the role — platform, community size, hours per week, and what you're looking for in a mod.

2

Review Applicants

Moderators apply with their experience and pitch. Review their profiles, ratings, and past engagement history.

3

Hire Your Moderator

Accept an applicant, agree on terms directly, and get your community moderated by someone you chose.

Who Are We

Mods4Hire is a marketplace purpose-built for online community management. We connect community owners, server admins, and platform operators with moderators who are actively looking for opportunities.

We built this because hiring moderation help is fragmented — listings end up buried in Discord servers or community forums with no standard process for vetting candidates or establishing accountability.

What We Do

We give hirers the tools to post structured listings with clear requirements — platform, community size, hours, and compensation — so moderators can find the right fit and apply with context.

We give moderators a way to build a verifiable public track record through a rating system that only activates after a confirmed engagement — so reputation means something.
