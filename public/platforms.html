All supported platforms

Mods4Hire supports moderation listings for any online community. Below are the platforms and environments where moderators are commonly placed. If your platform isn't listed, post a listing and select "Other" — our community covers a wide range of environments.

Communication & Social

Discord

Server moderation, channel management, bot oversight, role management, and community events.

Reddit

Subreddit moderation, post review, AutoModerator configuration, flair management, and community rules enforcement.

Twitch

Chat moderation, timeout and ban management, stream event coordination, and Twitch points oversight.

TeamSpeak

Server administration, channel hierarchy management, permission group setup, and voice moderation.

Forums & Communities

phpBB / XenForo / Discourse

Traditional forum platforms requiring thread moderation, spam removal, user ban management, and report handling.

Custom Forums

Proprietary or self-hosted community forums for games, brands, and organizations with custom moderation tooling.

Steam Discussions & Groups

Game hub moderation, Steam group management, community hub post review, and region-specific community oversight.

Facebook Groups

Group post approval, member request review, rule enforcement, and community growth moderation.

Game Servers & In-Game Admin

In-game administrators serve as a human enforcement layer alongside anti-cheat systems. They respond to player reports in real time, review evidence, issue context-aware bans, and help maintain fair competitive environments on game servers.

Garry's Mod Servers

DarkRP, TTT, PropHunt and other gamemode admins. Staff for rule enforcement, sit handling, and server health.

Rust Servers

Admin and moderator roles for private and community Rust servers. Cheat report handling, ban review, and wipe coordination.

Minecraft Servers

Staff for modding, player report handling, grief investigation, economy oversight, and event management.

FiveM / GTA Roleplay

Staff for RP rule enforcement, report resolution, whitelist management, and faction moderation on GTA V RP servers.

Ark: Survival Evolved / Ascended

Tribe conflict moderation, cheat investigation, dino and structure rollbacks, and server rule enforcement.

Other Game Servers

Private servers for any multiplayer game. If your game has a community server and a rule set, it needs moderation.

Other Platforms

Kick

Live stream chat moderation, channel subscriber management, and community rule enforcement.

YouTube Community

Live chat moderation during streams and premieres, comment review, and member management.

Don't see your platform?

Post a listing with "Other" selected and describe your platform. The right moderator is out there.
